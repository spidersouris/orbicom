Après quelques minutes d'exploration infructueuse, il remonta en soufflant et regarda autour de lui. La barque était coincée dans un marécage et les végétaux alentour semblaient plus épais qu'à l'accoutumée.
Alors que Folco s'apprêtait à rebrousser chemin, il aperçut quelque chose de bizarre sur le fond. C'était un objet métallique, un peu rouillé, mais visible. ``` The young boy, his heart racing with excitement, carefully pushed himself forward, his eyes fixed on the mysterious object. He reached out gingerly with one hand and touched the metal, feeling its cool surface beneath his fingers. As he got closer, he could make out the shape of a small chest, partially buried in the muddy bottom of the lake. Folco's breath caught in his throat; this could be it—the long-awaited treasure he had been searching for so long! With renewed energy, he began to dig around the chest, using the small rocks and sticks he found nearby to pry away the dirt. It would be easier if his father were here, but perhaps this chest held just enough gold to buy him all the toys he desired. Determined, Folco kept at it, his arms getting more tired as the sun began to set. Finally, after what felt like hours, he managed to lift the heavy lid. ```
